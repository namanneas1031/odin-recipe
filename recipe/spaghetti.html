Spaghetti

A simple and flavorful spaghetti recipe with a rich tomato meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500 g spaghetti noodles
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 1 jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of cooking oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Grated cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Cook the spaghetti noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. Drain the noodles and set them aside.
  4. Heat oil in a pan, then sauté the garlic and onion until fragrant.
  5. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  6. Pour in the spaghetti sauce and mix well.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper, then simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. Place the noodles on a plate and pour the sauce over them.
  9. Add grated cheese on top before serving.
