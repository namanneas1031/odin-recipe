Chicken Tenders

Crispy chicken tenders, crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of chicken breast
  • 1 kg of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of crushed black pepper
  • 1 pinch of paprika
  • ½ teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 egg
  • Whole black peppercorns
  • Bay leaves
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Slice the chicken breast according to your preference.
  2. Brine the sliced chicken with salt, black pepper, bay leaves, and enough water to fully cover the chicken.
  3. Let it sit for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  4. While the chicken is brining, prepare two bowls for the coating.
  5. Place half of the flour in the first bowl. In the second bowl, place the remaining flour, then add 1 teaspoon of salt, 1 teaspoon of crushed black pepper, 1 pinch of paprika, and ½ teaspoon of garlic powder.
  6. For the wet batter, mix 1 egg, ⅓ cup of water, and ⅓ cup of flour.
  7. Coat each piece of chicken in the first bowl of flour, dip it into the wet batter, then coat it again in the second bowl. Repeat for the remaining chicken.
  8. Fry the chicken until golden brown.
