Chicken Adobo

A classic Filipino chicken adobo cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ⅓ cup vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 6 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon whole black peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar, optional

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken in a bowl and add the soy sauce and garlic.
  2. Marinate the chicken for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and lightly brown the chicken.
  4. Add the marinade, water, bay leaves, and peppercorns.
  5. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  6. Add the vinegar and do not stir for about 3 minutes.
  7. Lower the heat, cover the pan, and simmer for 30 to 40 minutes.
  8. Add sugar if you want a slightly sweeter adobo.
  9. Cook until the chicken is tender and the sauce has reduced.
  10. Serve with hot rice.
